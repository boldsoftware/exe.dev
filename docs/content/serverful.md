---
title: Persistent disks, not serverless
description: exe.dev is serverful, not serverless
subheading: "3. Editorials"
---

For productivity, the bain of most Platform-as-a-Service offerings,
are the hallmark of "serverless": the lack of a persistent disk.

At exe.dev, your box comes with a normal, boring file system. Run a database
on it. Use sqlite. Store files.

Immutable infrastructure has its place, but it's not the only way to go.
