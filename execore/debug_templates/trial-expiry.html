Trial Expiry Enforcer

/debug > trial-expiry

Settings

Info: When enabled, the enforcer automatically stops VMs belonging to users whose trial plans have expired. It sleeps until the next trial expiry, then checks each expired user via the full plan resolution (so users who subscribed, joined a team, or have friend/VIP status are never affected). The rate limit controls how frequently VMs are stopped (e.g. one user every 10 minutes).
