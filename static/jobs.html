We are hiring in the Bay Area.

Join a team that cares deeply about internet infrastructure, finding a place for all of our AI agents to work, and designing products that developers love.

All roles are in the Bay Area. We come into the office three times a week in SF.

Open roles

  • Infrastructure engineering: deep expertise with systems programming, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure. We use technology like crosvm/clh and cgroups, and prefer to make system calls ourselves rather than pile products atop one another.
  • Backend engineering: use Go to make everything work, from ssh to billing to auth and identity. Your job is to know how it all works and be willing and able to get it done.
  • Frontend engineer: you will own the UI for our entire frontend, from web app to command line REPL. If a user experiences it, you are in charge of that experience. We expect a strong understanding of the fundamentals: HTML/CSS/JS, demonstrable visual taste, and joy in making things feel good. The rest is in your hands.

Write to david@bold.dev.

Compensation

Salary and equity are competitive. Salaries range from $150-200k. We just raised a seed round, so equity is at least 0.2%.
